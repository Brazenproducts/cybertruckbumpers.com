Why Skid Plates Matter on the Cybertruck

The Cybertruck's battery pack sits low in the chassis, making underbody protection critical for any off-road use. A rock strike to the battery pack can be catastrophic — skid plates and underbody armor are not optional for serious off-road Cybertruck owners. Even for daily drivers, skid plates protect against speed bumps, parking lot obstacles, and road debris.
